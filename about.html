Who am I?

I'm a 23-year-old Gemini residing in the heart of Texas. I graduated in 2018 with a Communications degree with a minor in Psychology from Texas A&M International University. I'm a creative maverick. I thrive in environments where creativity is encouraged. I love a good challenge, and I'm always down to learn new things. More on that on my resume.

I moved to Austin for its fresh perspective and its funky vibe. Although I might be new to the city I think I can already call it home. Some of my interests are fashion, music, and comedy. Some of my hobbies are painting, photography, going to concerts and watching stand up comedy.

I'm ready for anything!
